White Rice

Description

White rice is the go to in any household. You can literally eat rice with anything and will make the perfect comfort dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of white rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley (optional)
  • 2-3 garlic cloves

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice until the water tuns clear.
  2. In a medium saucepan, bring the water to a boil. Add the salt, stir, and then add the rinsed and drained rice
  3. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let simmer for 20 minutes. Check it after 15 minutes to see if all the water has evaporated. If it has, the rice is ready. If not, replace the lid and let the rice simmer an additional 5 minutes.
  4. Remove from the heat and fluff with a fork. Transfer to a plate and serve garnished with butter and parsley, if desired.
